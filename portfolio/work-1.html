Rencotre Annuelle des Femmes de Proverbes

Aligned with God's Will to Manifest His Kingdom

This annual event honors women inspired by the values of Proverbs 31, who spend time in worship, prayer, and edification, focusing on the manifestation of God's power in every sphere of their lives.

Through impactful messages and practical workshops, participants will discover how to embody wisdom and virtue to spiritually and socially impact those around them.
